Post to All Social Media From Your Phone, With One App

For UK tradespeople who want to share the job they just finished to every channel, straight from the phone in their pocket.

The best moment to post about a job is the second you finish it, while the photo is fresh and you are stood right there. That moment happens on site, with a phone, not later at a desk. So the real question is not "how do I post to social media", it is "how do I post to all of it from my phone in under a minute". Here is how.

The problem with posting from your phone the normal way

Doing it the usual way means a chain of small jobs. Open Facebook, write the post, attach the photo. Close it. Open Instagram, write it again, attach the photo again. Close it. Open LinkedIn, do it once more. By the third app you are loading the van and the idea of posting to the other eight has quietly died.

That app-hopping is why most trades end up on one platform only, or stop posting altogether. The phone is not the issue. Doing the same task over and over is.

The better way: one app that posts everywhere

The fix is a single app where you write the update once and it goes to all your channels at the same time, from the phone, on site. No switching between apps, no retyping, no reattaching photos. You do the bit only you can do, describing the job, and the app does the repetitive part.

How to post to every platform from your phone

1. Open the app on your phone. Standing by the finished work, with the photo you just took.
2. Write one plain line about the job. Something like "Full rewire finished today in Leeds, tidy job, happy customer." Add the photo.
3. Choose your platforms. Tick the channels you want this one to land on. Leave any off you do not want.
4. Tap post. The same update goes out, formatted for each platform, across all of them at once.

The whole thing is quicker than writing a single post the old way, and you walk away present on every channel instead of one.
